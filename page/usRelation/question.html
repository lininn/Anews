When was VST established?

A: VST was established in Hong Kong since 1991

When did VST list on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (HKEx)?

VST was listed on the HKEx main board in 2002

What is VST’s business?

VST is the leading Information and Communications Technology (ICT) products and services provider. The company runs over HKD 45 billion business and partnership with over 140 leading global IT vendors. With a network of more than 35,000 active channel partners, including IT malls, IT and electronic super-mart and retailers, covering China, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia and the Philippines.
